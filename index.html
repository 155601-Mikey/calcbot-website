menu_book Commands

/ping
Check if the bot is online.
/math [expression]
Solve math expressions or equations
Examples: /math 2+2, /math x+6=8, /math √4, /math π*2, /math 3²
/plot [equation]
Plot a mathematical function
Example: /plot y=x**2
/convert [query]
Convert units or currencies
Examples: /convert 10 cm to inches, /convert USD to EUR
/history
View your recent math history.
/setvar [name] [value]
Define custom variables
Example: /setvar a 5
/help
Get this help message.
